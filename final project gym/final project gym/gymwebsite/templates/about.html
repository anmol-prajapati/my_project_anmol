Our Story

Founded in 2010, PowerGym has been at the forefront of fitness innovation. We believe that everyone deserves access to world-class fitness facilities and expert guidance to achieve their health and wellness goals.

Our state-of-the-art facility spans over 15,000 square feet and features the latest in fitness technology, from high-tech cardio machines to Olympic-standard weightlifting equipment.
