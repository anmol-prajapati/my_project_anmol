About Rashtriya Gokul Mission:

Conserving indigenous bovine breeds and enhancing milk productivity across India.

Who We Are

The AI-Based Auto Recording of Animal Type Classification System is designed to modernize and streamline the evaluation of cattle and buffaloes under the Rashtriya Gokul Mission (RGM). Traditionally, Animal Type Classification (ATC) involves trained personnel visually examining and measuring physical traits of animals, and recording scores in the Bharat Pashudhan App (BPA). This manual process is prone to human error, fatigue, and bias, which can affect the reliability of data.

Our solution leverages Artificial Intelligence and image processing to automatically analyze animal images, extract key body structure parameters such as body length, height at withers, chest width, and rump angle, and generate standardized classification scores. By integrating with BPA, this system ensures accurate, consistent, and objective records, improving scientific evaluation and breeding decisions while minimizing human intervention.

Our Mission

To modernize and streamline Animal Type Classification in cattle and buffaloes by leveraging AI and image processing, ensuring accurate, consistent, and objective evaluation of physical traits. Our goal is to support scientific breeding programs under the Rashtriya Gokul Mission, reduce human error, and empower farmers and breeders with reliable data for improved productivity and genetic advancement.
